Our Services

Comprehensive neuropsychological evaluations tailored to your child's specific needs.

ADHD Evaluations

Comprehensive assessment for attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder, including differential diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

  • • Clinical interviews and rating scales
  • • Continuous performance testing
  • • Executive function assessment

Learning Disability Assessment

Evaluation for specific learning disorders in reading, writing, and mathematics with detailed educational recommendations.

  • • Academic achievement testing
  • • Cognitive processing evaluation
  • • IEP/504 plan recommendations

Autism Spectrum Evaluation

Comprehensive assessment for autism spectrum disorder using gold-standard diagnostic tools and clinical observation.

  • • ADOS-2 administration
  • • Developmental history assessment
  • • Social communication evaluation

Concussion & Brain Injury

Post-concussion evaluation and monitoring of cognitive recovery following traumatic brain injury.

  • • Baseline and post-injury testing
  • • Return-to-play evaluations
  • • Academic accommodation planning
